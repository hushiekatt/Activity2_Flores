About Myself

I am Hannah Katrina G. Flores, a college student at the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines. Currently, I am studying Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, nearing completion of my second year. I chose this course based on my parents' suggestion, as they observed my aptitude for resolving minor technological issues. They are not tech-savvy individuals themselves, and they believed that enrolling me in this course would help me develop professional IT skills. They also suggested this course because of its great demand and the expectation of rich career prospects and well-paying jobs in the IT industry. I actually wanted to pursue a course that would align with my dreams of becoming a professional artist. Art has been my passion since I was around 2 years old, but recognizing that it is not widely recognized as a viable career option in this country, I decided to pursue it as a sideline. Unfortunately, I wasn't aware of other courses that specialize in the arts, which led me to choose Information Technology as my main field of study.
